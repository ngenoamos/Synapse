💡 What is SRS Score?

SRS (Scoring & Ranking System) evaluates wallet risk based on:

  • 💰 Balance size and transaction volume
  • 📊 Transaction count and activity patterns
  • 🔐 Contract vs EOA (externally owned account)
  • ⭐ Historical on-chain behavior

Score Range: 0-100 (Higher = Lower Risk)

✅ 80-100: Low Risk | ⚠️ 50-79: Medium Risk | 🔴 0-49: High Risk
